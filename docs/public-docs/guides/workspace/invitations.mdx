---
title: Invitations
description: Invite collaborators to your Traseq workspace and manage invitation status.
---

Workspace Owners and Admins can invite collaborators to join the workspace.

## Send an invitation

1. Go to **Settings > Invitations**.
2. Click **Invite Member**.
3. Enter the person's email address.
4. Choose a role: `Admin`, `Member`, or `Viewer`.
5. Click **Send Invitation**.

## Invitation status

| Status       | Description                                |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------ |
| **Pending**  | Sent — waiting for the recipient to accept |
| **Accepted** | The person has joined the workspace        |
| **Expired**  | The invitation was not accepted in time    |
| **Revoked**  | The invitation was cancelled               |

## Accept an invitation

1. Click the invitation link in the email.
2. Sign in to Traseq (or create an account).
3. Confirm to join the workspace.

## Revoke an invitation

If you need to cancel a pending invitation:

1. Find the invitation in the list.
2. Click **Revoke**.
